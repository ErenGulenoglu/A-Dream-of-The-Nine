**G'eldfryn Do'ghym** was a drow **rogue** and an old adventuring companion of [[Leman of Al’Var]]. He aided Leman by bringing him rare magical artifacts and materials from the Underdark—items seldom found on the surface. G'eldfryn maintained connections with several influential drow families.
