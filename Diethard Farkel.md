**Baron Diethard Farkel** was the baron of western [[Patria]] and a member of [[House Farkel]]. He was born in 805 AA and inherited the barony in 837 AA. As the sole heir of his family during _the War of the Flames_, he remained at his manor in [[Turstinhill Village]] rather than taking the field.

After his father, **Baron Patryk Farkel**, passed away in 836 AA, Diethard succeeded to the title. In the winter of 837 AA, when their liege lord, [[House Olmin]], died out without heirs, he took the initiative to visit their ancestral seat at [[Wolfkeep]] and sent letters to the regency in [[Ewyngard]] concerning the defense of western Patria until the succession was resolved. He was temporarily granted the authority to raise local militias.

In the fall of 838 AA, after the defeat at the [[Battle of Embers]], several western Patrian nobles rebelled in an attempt to gain autonomy from the regency in Ewyngard. Baron Diethard Farkel took command of the garrison at Heartkeep along with local militias and launched a series of surprise attacks against the rebels before they could unite.

After the SPQU was formed in 838 AA, he was formally recognized as Baron of western Patria, granting his family authority over the western lands of the province and the hereditary lordship of [[Heartkeep]]. In 851 AA, his first wife, **Margarete Farkel**, died in childbirth. In the summer of 856 AA, he married [[Elena Pond]], receiving a handsome dowry from the _Green Pond_ branch of [[House Pond]].

In the summer of 857 AA, he summoned the noble houses of western Patria to Wolfkeep to hear their cases.
