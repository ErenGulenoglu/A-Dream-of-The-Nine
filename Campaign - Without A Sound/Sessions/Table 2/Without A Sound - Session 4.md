## Scene 1: Finding the Party
- Athena returns to Salutarius camp. After some time, Sialis returns to the camp, hardly walking and in bandages.
- Sialis thanks Athena for her help, and rewards her with 10 gp given by Lord Orvyll.
	- Sialis tells her that there twelve died on the road, but most survives. They barely managed to get out of the forest in the last push.
	- Sialis is more worried about another matter. She asked towns guard about comings and goings of [[Robin Haddock]] and her party, but no one returned since the last five days. Sialis is too weak to travel inside the forest, she asks Athena to find what happened to them.
		- **Haddock**, Robin's father isn't around as well.
	- Sialis tells her that she learned from [[Doran of Greentown]] that he set the party that helped her in the forest to find medicine for Lord Vincentas.
	- Athena can find the party with **DC 5** Survival check. The group's travel speed was slow, so Athena finds them half an hour away from the town in the forest.
## Scene 2: Lost Ones

	The tracks of the prior party takes you further inside the forest. After an hour and a half, you smell a strong odor that is inescapable. Across a small and shallow lake on your path, you see four bodies on the ground, laying side by side.
- The corpses belong to **Robin Haddock**, [[Helen Nolast]], [[Tommen of Al'Var]], and [[Cran Nolast]].
	- The bodies are looted to their boots. Medicine of the sick lord is missing.
	- They are pale, composing, and there are visible bloating. Robin has an arrow that pierced her lung, and others sustained many slashing wounds.
- **DC 10** Survival check reveals a track near the bodies that goes deeper inside the forest that is apparently was used many times. This track goes to [[Burned Claws Scout Camp]].
## Scene 3: Bells for the Dead
- If party returns with the news of perishing of the prior party, [[Father Quinten]] comes to them asking if they brought bodies back or buried them.
	- If the party brings the bodies back, Father Quinten organizes a burial for the dead, and rings the bell for four times, each ring for the each fallen one who fought to defend the righteous children of the Eight.
## Scene 4: Burned Claws Scout Camp
- Through following goblin tracks in the forest, the party reaches Burned Claws Scout Camps.
	- Scene instructions are on its own page.
	- It takes 5 hours to travel to the scout camp from King's Stand.

- The news of party destroying one of the outposts of the goblins reaches to Lord [[Vincas Orvyll]]. Lord Orvyll sends [[Doran of Greentown]] to call the party to meet with them, and rewards each with 25 gp worth precious stones.
