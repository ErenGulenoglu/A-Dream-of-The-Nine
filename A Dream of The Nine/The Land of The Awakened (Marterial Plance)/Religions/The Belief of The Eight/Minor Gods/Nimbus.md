**Nimbus**, _the Painter of Clouds_, is a local lesser deity of [[The Twins|the Twins]], worshipped near villages at the foot of the [[Broken Mountains]] in [[Riverlands]], such as [[Under Mountain Village]]. The people believe that being close to the clouds is favorable in the eyes of Nimbus.

They govern the ever-shifting patterns of the clouds. Nimbus paints the clouds differently each time to govern the ever-shifting pattern of the Twins in the skies.

Worshippers of Nimbus read cloud patterns, rain, and lightning in the sky to understand their will. As a way of worshipping Nimbus to curry their favor, devotees climb to great heights during thunder storms. During this event, it is believed that one will hear and see Nimbus in the clouds and receive answers to their prayers.
