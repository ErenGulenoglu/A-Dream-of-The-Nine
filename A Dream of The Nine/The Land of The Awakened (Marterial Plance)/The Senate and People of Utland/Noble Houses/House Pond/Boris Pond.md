**Boris Pond** was a noble of [[House Pond]] and a member of the cadet branch known as _Green Pond_. He was born in the winter of 821 AA and was the second-born son of **Marvin Pond**. During _the War of the Flames_, he helped his family govern [[Blue Pond]] until [[Sir Maryn Pond]] returned from the war. He was exiled along with the rest of his siblings after his brother enraged **Ser Maryn Pond** by demanding the inheritance of House Pond.
