**Zahar Pond** was a noble of [[House Pond]], and leader of the cadet branch of family known as _Green Pond_. He was born in 815 AA's winter, and was the firstborn son of **Marvin Pond**.

In 836 AA, when [[House Pond#^MarynPond|Sir Maryn Pond]] and his sons; **Sir Garibald**, **Sir Gelther**, and **Sir Gerwald** went off to war, he took over his uncle's responsibilities with his father, and governed [[Blue Pond]], [[Appleton]], and [[Village of Blue Pond]]. Changing tax policies of House Pond, that favored commoners for centuries, he amassed wealth out of private business practices in Blue Pond until 840 AA until Sir Maryn Pond returned from the _War of the Flames.

When his father, **Marvin Pond** passed away in 851 AA, Zahar inherited his father's funds and responsibility to take care of his siblings. He suggested to his uncle, Sir Maryn Pond to choose him as his heir for the house, claiming that his young cousin is not ready to inherit the responsibilities if the health of his old uncle goes bad. Enraged on Zahar's insolence, Sir Maryn Pond has ousted Zahar and his siblings from [[Knight's Rest]].
