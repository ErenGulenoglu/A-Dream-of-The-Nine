**Idil Pond** was a noble of [[House Nevenir]]. She was born in 771 AA. In 784 AA, she married her betrothed, [[House Pond#^MarynPond|Ser Maryn Pond]].

She bore four sons: **Garibald Pond**, born in 799 AA; **Gelther Pond**, born in 802 AA; **Gerwald Pond**, born in 806 AA; and [[Gregory Pond]], born in 834 AA.

Her first three sons died during _the War of the Flames_ at the [[Battle of Embers]] in 838 AA. In 840 AA, Lady Idil Pond passed away.
