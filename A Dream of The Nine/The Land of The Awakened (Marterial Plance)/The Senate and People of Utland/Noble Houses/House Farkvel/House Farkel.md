**House Farkel** is a noble house and barony that governs western [[Patria]]. Their seat, inherited from their liege lord [[House Olmin]], [[Wolfkeep]], is located at the heart of western Patria. Historically, the family rose from a local barony of [[Turstinhill Village]] to provincial governance in 388 AA, after taking the initiative to suppress rebels in western Patria following _the War of Flames_. Their coat of arms is a gauntlet clenched into a fist on a red field. Their motto is “Our Hands Do Not Falter.”
