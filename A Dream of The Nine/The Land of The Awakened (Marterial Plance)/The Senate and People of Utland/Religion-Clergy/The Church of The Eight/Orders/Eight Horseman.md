The Eight Horseman are horseman, an order who dedicate themselves to [[The Church of The Eight|the Church of the Eight]]. Their oaths are different from knighthood oaths than that are taken by ones who serve to aristocrats. They only serve to the Eight. They were founded and initiated by [[Lucron]], as selected esteemed warriors of the fate. They defend the church from accusations with their weapons and armor, and command the clergy initiated in [[The Fighter|the Fighter]] in the field in times of crisis. It is said that Lucron anointed 8 of the capable warriors who were among the first believers to defend the free will in the name of the Eight. They serve as teachers in arms and faith, bodyguards to high ranked clergy, leaders in battles, and champions of the church in trials.
