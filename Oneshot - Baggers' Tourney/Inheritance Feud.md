**The Party Composition**
This adventure is designed for 1-2 players who act as a unified team. The players should build their characters around the following concepts:
- **The Participant:** At least one player **must** be a **Human Noble**. They are the official entrant in the tourney records.
- **The Partner:** The second player (optional) is a close ally of the Participant. They are not a rival, but a trusted teammate.

**Character Concepts**
When designing your characters, focus on your **shared bond**. You are a single unit representing your family name or interests within the [[The Senate and People of Utland|SPQU]]. Possible pairings include:
- **Cousins or Siblings:** Two nobles representing their house together. Both may compete in the brackets if both are Human Nobles.
- **Knight & Squire:** A noble and their trusted trainee/attendant working together to secure victory.

**Rules and Restrictions**
- **Lineage:** At least one player character **must** be a Human of Noble birth.
- **Regional Origin:** Characters should hail from the close geography of [[Patria]] or have a valid reason for traveling to this specific northern province.

### Character Concepts

When designing your characters, consider their relationship to the host province.

- **The Lead:** You are the face of your house. Your entry into the tourney is a matter of family honor or political maneuvering within the [[The Senate and People of Utland|SPQU]].
    
- **The Companion:** You are tied to the Lead’s success. Whether you are sharpening their blade or managing their social calendar, your fate in [[Patria]] is linked to theirs.
